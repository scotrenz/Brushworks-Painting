Services

BrushWorks Painting strives to provide exceptionally high quality interior painting and wallcoverings for both residential and commercial clients on a timely basis. Services include (but are not limited to):
  • Custom interior painting
  • Wall repair and preparation
  • Water damage repair
  • Wallpaper removal and installation
  • Staining and refinishing
  • Moulding installation services also available
